# SEO Content & E-E-A-T

Use this skill to evaluate content quality and ensure it meets Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) standards.

## Goal

Improve ranking by creating high-quality, helpful, and reliable content that satisfies user intent.

## Steps

1. **User Intent Analysis**:
   - Identify the primary keyword and intent (Informational, Navigational, Transactional).
   - Check if the content directly answers the user's likely question.
2. **E-E-A-T Evaluation**:
   - **Experience**: Check for first-hand usage or case studies.
   - **Expertise**: Verify author bios, credentials, and depth of knowledge.
   - **Authoritativeness**: Check for industry recognition and backlinks.
   - **Trustworthiness**: Verify contact info, HTTPS, privacy policy, and factual accuracy.
3. **Keyword Optimization**:
   - Ensure the primary keyword is in the Title, H1, and first paragraph.
   - Use semantic (LSI) keywords naturally.
   - Avoid keyword stuffing.

## Hard Stops

- Do not use AI-generated content without human refinement and factual verification.
- Do not hide author identities.
- Do not let thin content (low word count with no value) remain indexed.

## Checklist

- [ ] Clear primary H1
- [ ] Meaningful internal links
- [ ] No grammar/spelling errors
- [ ] Fast answers (above the fold)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized readability
